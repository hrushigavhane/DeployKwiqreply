MMS Converter

Messages sent with a MediaUrl to a recipient whose carrier does not support MMS can be sent as an SMS with a shortened URL linking to your media. The shortened URL link (*URL* followed by 6 unique characters) will be appended to the end of the SMS message body.

Messages to a recipient whose carrier cannot be reached via MMS will be sent as an SMS with a shortened URL linking to your media.

Messages to a recipient whose carrier cannot be reached via MMS will not be sent. Your application will receive an HTTP 400 response and your message will not be queued for delivery
